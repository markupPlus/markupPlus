Shopify

Shopify is a popular eCommerce platform that enables businesses to create their online stores with ease and finesse. Key to its functionality is the Content Management System (CMS), which serves as the backbone providing users with the ability to manage, edit, and publish different types of content.

The CMS part of Shopify enables users to update their website content without heavy reliance on tech skills. By offering a variety of templates, it allows users to customize their store's appearance, manage the layout, and continually update their website content – whether that be photos, or product descriptions.
